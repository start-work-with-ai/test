Travel Routes & Introductions

Detailed itineraries to help you plan your perfect adventure.

Paris Walking Tour Route

Discover the romance and history of Paris on foot. This carefully curated route takes you from iconic landmarks to hidden gems only locals know about.

📏 Total: 5.2 km ⏱ Duration: 3 hours 💪 Difficulty: Easy

Itinerary

  1. Eiffel Tower — Start your journey at the iconic iron tower with panoramic city views.
  2. Champ de Mars — Stroll through beautiful gardens with a backdrop of the Eiffel Tower.
  3. Seine River Walk — Walk along the scenic riverbank past historic bridges and bookstalls.
  4. Louvre Museum — Marvel at the glass pyramid and world-famous art collection.
  5. Notre-Dame Cathedral — End at this Gothic masterpiece on Île de la Cité.

Rome History & Food Route

Combine ancient history with culinary delights on this unforgettable Rome tour. Taste authentic cuisine while exploring millennia of civilization.

📏 Total: 6.8 km ⏱ Duration: 4 hours 💪 Difficulty: Easy

Itinerary

  1. Colosseum — Begin at the ancient amphitheater, a symbol of Imperial Rome.
  2. Roman Forum — Walk through the heart of ancient Roman public life.
  3. Trastevere District — Enjoy authentic Roman pasta and gelato in this charming neighborhood.
  4. Pantheon — Admire the perfectly preserved ancient temple and its magnificent dome.
  5. Trevi Fountain — End by tossing a coin into the baroque masterpiece.

Tokyo After Dark Route

Experience Tokyo's electric nightlife scene. From neon-lit streets to intimate izakayas, discover the city that never sleeps.

📏 Total: 4.5 km ⏱ Duration: 3 hours 💪 Difficulty: Easy

Itinerary

  1. Shibuya Crossing — Start at the world's busiest pedestrian crossing as it lights up at night.
  2. Shinjuku Golden Gai — Explore narrow alleys filled with tiny, themed bars.
  3. Kabukicho District — Experience the vibrant entertainment district with dazzling neon lights.
  4. Hidden Izakayas — Savor local snacks and sake in authentic Japanese-style pubs.
  5. Tokyo Tower Night View — End with a breathtaking panoramic view of the illuminated cityscape.

Hong Kong Highlights Route

Explore the vibrant fusion of East and West in Hong Kong. From soaring skyscrapers to ancient temples, bustling markets to tranquil harbours, this route showcases the best of Asia's World City.

📏 Total: 8.5 km ⏱ Duration: 5 hours 💪 Difficulty: Moderate

Itinerary

  1. Victoria Peak — Begin with a scenic Peak Tram ride for breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline and harbour.
  2. Star Ferry Harbour Crossing — Enjoy the iconic ferry ride across Victoria Harbour with stunning waterfront scenery.
  3. Temple Street Night Market — Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of street food stalls, fortune tellers, and local vendors.
  4. Wong Tai Sin Temple — Visit this iconic Taoist temple known for its beautiful architecture and fortune-telling traditions.
  5. Tsim Sha Tsui Promenade — End with the spectacular Symphony of Lights show over the harbour skyline.
